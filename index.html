Who are we?

Agrinect is a sustainable enterprise that aims to reduce food speculation and commercial farmers' cost of marketing and transportation of farm produce in Western and Southern Africa by connecting farmers with their target market making Agrinect a huge intermediary.

What do we do?

Marketing

Engage your customers with relevant conversations and timely notifications in channels where they are spending the most time.

E-commerce

Enable convenient buying experiences with text messaging. Drive revenue through messaging and voice channels by providing a new level of convenience.

Transportation

Deliver your products to your customers faster to better their experience.

How can we help?

We are an innovative enterprise aimed at designing and providing an innovative digital-based platform that offers multi-channel e-commerce, marketing and transportation solutions to farmers as well as commercial farmers in Western and Southern Africa

We provide farmers with an online platform that combines artificial intelligence, social media, web, mobile app platform and logistics to provide multi-channel e-commerce, marketing and transportation solutions for smallholder farmers. Through the platform, farmers (sellers) can better connect to buyers.
